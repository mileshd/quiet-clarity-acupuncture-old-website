I am licensed in the state of Maryland and recognized by major health insurers as a provider of acupuncture. I was a member of the TAI clinical faculty for 5 years, and helped pioneer an acupuncture treatment program for people recovering from drug addiction in Baltimore.

I have studied Zero Balancing since 1993 and was certified in 1995. In addition to my practice, I am also a mentor, assisting ZB candidates in the certification process.

I am a clinical faculty member of Ongiara College of Acupuncture and Moxibustion since its inception.

I have studied Five Element acupuncture extensively with Niki Bilton since 1999 to deepen my understanding of this system of healing. I am a devoted teacher and practitioner of Five Element principles. I combine my formal educational training with a strong spiritual practice.
